FAQ's

Why Should I Have a Website for My Business?

Every business needs a website to establish an online presence. A well-designed website showcases your products or services, attracts new customers, and increases revenue. It's a valuable platform for reaching a wider audience and promoting your business effectively.

How Can Mycoes Help Me with Web Development?

Mycoes offers a range of web development services, including frontend and backend development. We use popular technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Django, and MySQL to create customized and secure websites. Whether you need a complete website design or assistance with specific aspects, we're here to help.

What Development Technologies Does Mycoes Use?

Mycoes uses a combination of frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and Tailwind CSS. For backend development, we leverage Python, Django, MySQL, and RESTful APIs. These technologies ensure that your website is secure, customizable, and scalable.

How Does the Pricing Work?

We don't set fixed prices for our services. Instead, we encourage you to make an offer based on your budget and project requirements. If we agree on your offer, we'll inform you via email, and you can track your project's status through our platform. Let's have a discussion before finalizing your offer.

Are You a Freelancer or an Agency?

We are freelancers, not a large agency. Our goal is to provide high-quality web development services at an affordable price. We understand that small businesses and startups may have budget constraints, and we're here to help them establish their online presence without breaking the bank.

How Do I Get Started with Mycoes?

To get started with Mycoes, follow these steps: Explore our services, including frontend, backend, and fullstack development. Contact us to discuss your project needs and services you require. Make an offer based on your budget and project understanding. Track the progress of your project through our status navigation. Once the project is complete, make the payment as discussed, and you'll receive your project files.
